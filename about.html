About me

I am a final-year PhD candidate in the Innovation, Technology Entrepreneurship, and Marketing (ITEM) Group at Eindhoven University of Technology. My research interests broadly relate to human factors in software engineering; my current dissertation research investigates cognitive styles, specifically intuition and rationality, in software design decision making. I also have interest and experience in the domains of decision making, decision support, and web programming, particularly with regards to cognition. My promoter is Prof.Dr Fred Langerak, and my daily supervisors are Dr Maryam Razavian and Dr Katrin Eling.

 

I received my Master's degree (2016), Bachelor's (Honors) degree (2012), and Bachelor's degree in Socio-Informatics from the Department of Information Science at Stellenbosch University, Stellenbosch, South Africa. During my studies, I received a National Research Foundation (NRF) Scarce Skills scholarship (2012), a prestigious Harry Crossley Foundation scholarship (2013), and various merit bursaries from Stellenbosch University. Here, I also worked as a lecturer's assistant / tutor (2011 - 2012), and as a junior lecturer (2013 - 2016). My teaching specialisations, for which I was responsible lecturer, were web programming and database systems design. I also have experience teaching python programming, IT project management, technology in society, and enterprise systems.

 

In my spare time, I like to walk or take a run through the forests and parks in Eindhoven, or to do some gardening on my balcony. Although I am not a particularly fast runner, I am passionate about the sport (particularly inclusivity) and am part of the core volunteer team of Karpendonkse Plas parkrun. I am also currently a volunteer at HOST Eindhoven, an organization that endeavors to provide a "home away from home" for international students in Eindhoven. When the situation allows, I really like to travel and learn about other cultures and places, and occasionally attempt to take interesting photos while doing so. Finally, I enjoy wine and craft beer tasting, fine dining, and cooking delicious food when I'm feeling adventurous.
